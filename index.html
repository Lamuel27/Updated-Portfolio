About Me

My name is Sam Rehlinger and I am in the process of becoming a Full-Stack Developer. I hope to use what I have learned to better my career in the software industry.

I grew up in Marshfield, WI and graduated from the University of Wisconsin - Stout with a degree in Business Administration. The girl next to me in the photo is my beautiful fiance. We recently got engaged at the end of August. It is great to have her support throughout this course, especially since we cannot travel on weekends for the next 6 months.

I currently work at a Fintech company called ClickSWITCH. ClickSWITCH is an automated account switching solution that revolutionizes the way financial institutions attract and retain new primary account holders. This unique customer acquisition technology automates, streamlines and simplifies the process of bringing new account holders onboard. ClickSWITCH quickly, safely and efficiently switches account holders recurring payments from their old accounts to new ones. I believe I have a lot to contribute when it comes to building our application, so once I finish this course I hope to move to our development team.

Side note: My brother (Aaron Rehlinger) is also taking this course with me. So, that is pretty NEAT! Our family enjoys outdoor activities, such as biking.
